### Blackjack is a card game:

> - The goal is to get a hand value as close to 21 without exceeding it.

> - Players are initially dealt two cards, and the dealer also has two<br>cards, which one of them must be face down. 

> - Players can choose to "hit" to get another card or "stand" to keep their current total. 

> - Numbered cards are worth as their face value, but An ace can be worth either 1 or 11,<br>depending on the player's choice.
